Divinity: Original Sin

Divinity: Original Sin is the fifth game in the Divinity series, developed by Larian Studios. It is an isometric, single-player or cooperative multiplayer fantasy RPG with tactical turn-based combat. The game offers a highly interactive world with numerous choices and consequences that impact the game.

Game Features

The game is known for its tactical turn-based combat and deep interaction within its world. Players can explore freely, manipulate their environments, and use a variety of strategies to overcome challenges. Divinity: Original Sin is also a prequel to Divine Divinity, the first game in the series, enriching the franchise's storyline and setting.

Development Tools

The game was shipped with the Divinity Engine, the same editor that Larian Studios used to develop the game. This allows users to create their own single-player and multiplayer adventures and publish them online, fostering a community of content creators.

Funding and Release

Larian Studios launched a successful crowdfunding campaign on Kickstarter on 27 March 2013, raising over $1,000,000, including PayPal pledges, by 26 April 2013. This funding allowed the game to be made available via Steam's Early Access program before its full release on 30 June 2014.
